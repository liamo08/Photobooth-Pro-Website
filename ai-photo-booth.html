What is an AI photo booth?

An AI photo booth uses artificial intelligence to transform photos in real-time at events. Instead of just capturing photos, it can swap faces into magazine covers, remove and replace backgrounds, create artistic transformations, and more. BoothLedger includes two AI engines with 50+ creative effects.

How does AI face swap work?

BoothLedger's AI face swap detects faces in the captured photo and seamlessly places them into pre-designed scenes like magazine covers, movie posters, or custom branded images. The AI preserves facial features and expressions for realistic results.

Do I need a green screen for AI backgrounds?

No! BoothLedger's AI background removal works without a green screen. The AI automatically detects subjects and removes the background, replacing it with any scene you choose. This saves setup time and works in any venue.

How fast are the AI effects?

AI effects are processed in seconds. Guests see their transformed photo almost instantly, keeping the line moving at busy events.
