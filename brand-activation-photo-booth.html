Brand Activation Photo Booth
Common Questions

Do AI effects really drive more shares than regular photos?

Yes — novelty drives sharing. A regular booth photo gets sent in a private message. A magazine cover with the guest's face on it gets posted publicly. We see roughly 5× share rates on AI vs standard.

Can I pre-fill the share copy with a campaign hashtag?

Yes. Per-event share copy templates with hashtag, mention, and link.

Does it work offline at pop-ups?

Yes. Capture, AI processing (with on-device fallback), printing, and AirDrop/QR sharing all work offline. Cloud uploads when connected.

Can the brand get all the photos and data afterwards?

Yes — full event archive download (photos + leads CSV) at activation end. GDPR-friendly.

How does pricing work for agency use?

Per-device licenses with the AI Photobooth plan at EUR 35/month per 2 devices. Multi-device discounts available for tour deployments — talk to us.
