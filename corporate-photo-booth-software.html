FAQ

Corporate Photo Booth Software
Common Questions

Can I capture leads at the booth?

Yes — configurable form before sharing: name, email, custom fields. Required or optional, GDPR opt-in included.

How do I get the leads into our CRM?

CSV export from BoothLedger Cloud. Most CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive) accept CSV import. We don't push directly to avoid forcing a single integration.

Is it GDPR compliant?

BoothLedger gives you the tools (configurable consent, opt-in, export, deletion). Compliance ultimately depends on your event policies and how you handle exported data. We're a processor, you're the controller.

Can I customize the booth for each sponsor?

Yes. Save brand profiles per sponsor: templates, colours, logos, sharing copy, hashtags. Switch profiles per event.

Does it work for multi-day expos?

Yes. Remote management means you can update templates and check device status without an on-site operator every morning.
