What DSLR cameras does BoothLedger support?

Full Canon DSLR lineup with live view: Rebel/EOS series, 5D, 6D, 7D, 80D, 90D, R-series mirrorless, and most cameras supported by Canon EDSDK. Nikon and Sony support is on the roadmap.

Does BoothLedger work with Canon mirrorless cameras?

Yes — R, RP, R5, R6, R7, R8, R10, R50, R100 over USB tethered with live view, autofocus, exposure control.
