The Look

What Makes a
Glam Booth?

A glam booth is a lighting setup plus a processing recipe. The lighting blows out the background to clean white. The processing lifts skin tones, softens texture, drops to high-contrast monochrome, and crops tight. BoothLedger handles the recipe so you can focus on the experience.

Automated Glam Recipe

One-tap glam mode applies the full preset: skin smoothing tuned to preserve features, exposure lift to clip the white background, optional B&W or low-saturation color, and high-contrast tone curve. Adjust intensity per event from a single slider.

Skin Smoothing

Subtle, frequency-separation style smoothing. No plastic-skin look.

Background Pull

Clean white-out of softbox-lit backgrounds. Even forgives slightly off lighting.

B&W or Color

Toggle per-event. The Kim K aesthetic is high-contrast monochrome but warm-tone color works for some brands.

Branded Templates

Glam prints with sponsor logos, event hashtags, or wedding monograms. Single-image or strip layouts.

Setup

What You Need to
Run a Glam Booth

1. Canon DSLR

Any Canon BoothLedger supports. The look depends on real lens depth and a real sensor.

2. Two Soft Lights

Continuous LED panels or strobes through 60cm+ softboxes. Position 45° either side of subject.

3. White Backdrop

Seamless paper or fabric. Light it 1-2 stops brighter than the subject for the blown-out look.

4. BoothLedger

Glam preset, instant processing, branded prints, sharing — all in one app.

Common Questions

What is a glam booth?

A photo booth styled after Kim Kardashian's high-key B&W beauty photos. Soft, bright lighting + clean white background + high-contrast B&W processing.

What hardware do I need?

Canon DSLR + two soft lights (LED or strobe through softboxes) + white backdrop + Windows PC running BoothLedger + optional dye-sub printer.

Does BoothLedger automate the glam look?

Yes — one-tap preset for skin smoothing, exposure lift, B&W conversion, and background pull. Adjustable intensity.

How much does it cost?

From EUR 20/month for the Photo Booth plan with glam mode. EUR 35/month for AI add-ons.

Can I run a colour glam booth instead of B&W?

Yes — toggle B&W off, glam preset still applies skin smoothing, contrast and white-background pull.
