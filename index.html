Core Features

Everything You Need to Run
a Professional Photo Booth

BoothLedger is packed with features that professional event photographers need to deliver unforgettable experiences.

Camera Support

Canon DSLR integration with live view preview, autofocus, and high-resolution capture. Built-in webcam support as a flexible alternative. More camera brands coming soon.

Beautiful Templates

Design branded multi-photo layouts with photo slots, overlays, text, shapes, and QR codes. Landscape or portrait orientation with cloud-synced template management.

Professional Printing

Direct printing to dye-sublimation printers like the DNP DS-RX1. Configurable paper sizes, auto-print mode, and multiple printer support for high-volume events.

Instant Sharing

Guests share photos instantly via SMS (Twilio), email, QR codes, or cloud gallery links. Customizable message templates with private share URLs for every photo.

Real-Time Filters

Eight stunning live filters — Black & White, Sepia, Warm, Cool, Vivid, Muted, and Vintage — applied in real-time to the camera preview so guests see the effect before capture.

Boomerang / GIF Mode

Capture Instagram-style boomerang animations your guests will love sharing. Configurable duration, playback speed, and loop count. Works with both DSLR and webcam.

Offline Resilience

Works seamlessly without internet. Photos store locally and sync automatically when connectivity returns. Upload queue with retry ensures nothing is ever lost.

Event Management

Organize all photos by event with a browsable gallery. Portable folder structure makes backup simple. Track composites, raw captures, AI-generated images, and GIFs per event.
