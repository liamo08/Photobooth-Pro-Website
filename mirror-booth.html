The Concept

What is a
Mirror Booth?

A mirror booth is a full-length interactive mirror with a hidden display, camera, and touch screen. Guests see their reflection overlaid with animated prompts, tap the screen to trigger the photo capture, and receive instant prints or digital copies. It combines the elegance of a mirror with the fun of a photo booth — creating an experience that looks premium and keeps guests entertained.

Full-Length Display

Mirror booths typically use a 50–65 inch touch screen in portrait orientation, hidden behind two-way mirror glass. Guests see their reflection while the display shows animations and prompts.

DSLR Camera Quality

A Canon DSLR camera mounted behind the glass captures professional-quality photos. BoothLedger controls the camera directly for consistent exposure and focus at every event.

Touch Interaction

Guests tap the mirror to start, pose with on-screen props, sign their photo, and choose how to receive it. The touch interface makes the entire experience feel magical and intuitive.
