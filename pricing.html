Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when my trial ends?
When your 30-day trial ends, you'll be prompted to choose a plan. Your events and photos are preserved — just subscribe to regain access. No data is deleted.
What does "2 devices included" mean?
Each subscription covers up to 2 separate computers running BoothLedger. This is tracked automatically when you sign in. If you need to switch a device, contact support to free up a slot.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes. You can upgrade from Basic to AI Photobooth at any time. The price difference is prorated. You can also switch between monthly and yearly billing through the customer portal.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel anytime from your billing portal. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. No hidden fees or penalties.
Is there a free trial of the AI features?
The 30-day free trial includes basic features. AI features are available immediately when you subscribe to the AI Photobooth plan.
