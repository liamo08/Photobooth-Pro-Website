FAQ

Roaming Photographer Software
Common Questions

What hardware do I need for a roaming setup?

A Windows tablet (Surface, Lenovo, Dell — anything Win 10/11), a Canon DSLR with a wide-ish lens, a USB cable, and an on-camera flash. Sling strap for the tablet helps.

Can I do this on iPad?

Not with BoothLedger today — we're Windows-first. macOS coming. For iPad-based roaming, Snappic and similar exist.

Does it work without WiFi?

Yes. AirDrop and QR sharing work peer-to-peer. Cloud archive uploads when WiFi returns.

Can I apply AI effects on the fly?

Yes — on the AI Photobooth plan. Tap to apply vogue, magazine, sketch, movie poster. Roughly 5-15 seconds per effect depending on engine and connectivity.

How does this compare to a regular booth?

Booth sits still, guests come to it. Roaming goes to the guests — better for venues where you can't set up, mingling-style events, or premium service tier.
