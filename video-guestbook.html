The Modern Guestbook

What Is a
Video Guestbook?

A video guestbook is a dedicated recording station at your event where guests step up to a camera and leave a short video message for the host. Instead of scribbling a few words in a book that collects dust, guests share genuine reactions, heartfelt wishes, and funny stories — all captured in HD and saved to the cloud automatically.

More Than Words

A written message can say congratulations, but a video captures the smile, the tears of joy, and the tone of voice behind those words. Video guestbooks preserve emotions that paper never could.

Safe in the Cloud

Every recording is automatically uploaded to BoothLedger Cloud. No lost USB drives, no corrupted files. Access and download all video messages from any device after your event.

Quick and Easy

Guests tap a button, record their message, review it, and they are done. The intuitive full-screen interface needs no instructions — even grandparents can use it.

Questions Answered

What is a video guestbook?

A video guestbook is a modern alternative to a traditional paper guestbook. Instead of writing a message, guests step up to a camera and record a short video message for the host. BoothLedger captures these in HD quality and uploads them to the cloud for easy access after the event.

What equipment do I need for a video guestbook?

You need a Windows PC or laptop running BoothLedger and either a webcam or a Canon DSLR camera for higher quality recordings. A tripod and basic lighting are recommended. BoothLedger handles everything else including the recording interface, playback, and cloud upload.

How long can each video message be?

You can configure the maximum recording duration in BoothLedger's settings. Most events use 30 to 60 second recordings, but you can set any length that suits your event. A countdown timer shows guests how much recording time remains.

Can guests share their video messages instantly?

Yes. After recording, guests can share their video via QR code, SMS, or email directly from the booth. Videos are also uploaded to BoothLedger Cloud so the event host can access and download all messages in one place.

Is a video guestbook good for weddings?

Absolutely. A video guestbook captures emotions, laughter, and heartfelt messages that a paper guestbook simply cannot. Couples treasure video messages from their wedding day for years to come. BoothLedger makes it easy with custom intro screens, branding, and automatic cloud backup.
