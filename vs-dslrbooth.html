Where BoothLedger Wins

Why Operators Switch to

Built-In AI, No Add-Ons

Face swap, magazine covers, vogue, sketch, movie poster looks — all included on the AI Photobooth plan. dslrBooth users typically string together third-party services or pay extra for AI add-ons.

Cloud-First Architecture

Every photo syncs to BoothLedger Cloud automatically. Guests get personal galleries, you get a complete event archive, and you can manage every device remotely. dslrBooth pushes you to fotoShare as a separate product.

Two Devices Per License

BoothLedger plans include 2 device licenses by default at EUR 20/month. dslrBooth Pro is per-device, so a 2-booth operator pays roughly double right out of the gate.

One Vendor, One Bill

Software, cloud, AI, remote config, and printing are all in one subscription. dslrBooth + fotoShare + AI add-ons means multiple bills and multiple support inboxes.
